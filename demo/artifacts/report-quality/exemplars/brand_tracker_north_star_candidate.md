## Design Standard

- One action title per slide, fixed position and size.
- Two fonts maximum.
- Palette roles: text, neutral grid, positive movement, caution.
- Base/source note on every exhibit.
- Tables only where the exact number matters; otherwise use simple bars or small multiples.
